Tommy Hilfiger 1791370

Discover the distinctively relaxed style of this casual new men's sports watch. It features a 44mm stainless steel case with fluidly extended lugs. White-filled arrow-tipped skeleton hour and minute hands add another level of intrigue to this modern sports design on retro-inspired mesh bracelet. Water resistant to 5 ATM/50 meters.

    4.0 out of 5 starsSimple, stylish, substantial watch without lots of bells and whistles
    ByCarol C.VINE VOICEon July 1, 2017
    Vine Customer Review of Free Product( What's this? )
    This Tommy Hilfiger watch is simple and ataractic, and the Tommy Hilfiger logo and styling give it a nautical feel. The watch face is somewhat large and deep, about 5/8" deep. outer rim of the face contains a ring that is marked at every second, and it is actually sitting at an angle up about 45 degrees, rather than lying flat on the face. It's an interesting and unusual twist that gives the watch some style. The watch contains a large and small hand, decorated with white enamel and a bit of red and blue, a sweep second hand with a red tip, and a very small arrow that points to the inner dial. Although the inner ring looks like it would do something fancy -- it is just a 24 hour clock, and somewhat difficult to read at that because the little arrow is so small and doesn't have the bright white markings like the other hands. The small dial marks the corresponding time on a 24-hour clock. Most people I know who are old enough to use a watch can tell whether it is day or night and don't have much difficulty adding 12 to a number between 1 and 12 -- so I'm not sure the 24-hour clock and dial provides much value added. The extra enamel ring does make the watch look sportier. The band is surprising substantial. I have owned Skagen watches with stainless mesh bands and this seems much thicker and heavier than those. The mesh is super smooth and looks like it will hold up well. Overall, this is a nice looking, somewhat basic watch, combining simplicity and style.

    3.0 out of 5 starsGood looking but some issues.
    ByGo Blue 99VINE VOICEon July 18, 2017
    Vine Customer Review of Free Product( What's this? )
    I’ve gotten compliments on the sporty yet classy look of this watch. The bracelet is gorgeous, smooth and comfortable. The clasp is tight and easily adjustable. It is a very attractive basic watch.

    But there are some problems. The watch has no date, day, or chronometer. It doesn’t glow in the dark or illuminate. In a quiet room you can hear the second hand ticking. It has a dark face with easy to read numbers around the outer edge. It has an inner ring has two calibrations; one for 24 hour time, the other for seconds. The other end of the hour hand doubles as a pointer that points to the 24 hour dial. The 24 hour dial is offset 6 hours from the dial face, something that took a little getting used to. I never use the inner dial since I’d prefer the 12 and 24 hour dials to be the same, but the calibrations do look cool.

    I’ve seen similar Tommy watches with a chronometer sell for lower price than this watch, so I’m not sure why this watch cost more.
    I like to wear this watch for every casual occasion and for everyday use. It is a very attractive watch, and very basic.
